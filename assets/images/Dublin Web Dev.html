About Dublin Web Dev

We began after noticing the amount of small businesses in Dublin that have outdated websites, or even no websites at all.

We believe this is grave mistake for any business in the modern world, and we want to fix it, at an affordable cost!

We take care of designing, developing, hosting and maintaining websites, allowing business owners to focus on their business!

Crezo Construction
I made this site for a small construction company in Vancouver. I replaced their old Wordpress site with a more modern version using the latest technologies, like Gatsby.js.

Loading times were vastly decreased and Google Lighthouse audit scores improved dramatically in all areas. I followed the latest design trends to give the site a sleek new look, in line with their brand colours.

I linked the site to Contentful CMS so they can manage the content themselves, without knowing any code.
